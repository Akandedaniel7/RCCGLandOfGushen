Womens's Fellowship

The Glorious Women
ABOUT US

Who we are.

The Glorious Women’s Fellowship is a natural group in Jesus House, where women meet, interact, fellowship and learn at the feet of our Master Jesus Christ, and from one another.

We are by God’s grace, saved daughters of Zion, and by the mercy of God, sisters in Christ. Ours is a fellowship where the saved and unsaved are welcome and warmly received. We are home to many and a place of succour for one another. As a group of Christian ladies, we are bonded by the love of Christ for one another. We strive to serve one another and to look out for each other, we are INTENTIONAL about our relationship and fellowship with Christ and with one another. We rejoice and celebrate with those who rejoice and celebrate and also do our best at all times to support women as they go through different seasons of life. We seek to continually bring honour and glory to the Father. Indeed, we are the ‘Glorious Women!!!’ We welcome not only women who are members of the church, but also others who accept the “Purpose” of the Fellowship, and own her mission and vision.

“We are God’s handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do. – Ephesians 2:10”

What we do.

We basically fellowship with one another both real-time and virtual. We have a robust physical fellowship and a vibrant online fellowship. We study the word and share lessons learned. We also handle real-life issues and relationship matters as we look at scriptures for guidance.

As women, we understand our uniqueness and unique roles in the home, church, and society at large. We treat topics that capture these essences. We minister to the whole, total and complete woman, by tackling her spiritual, physical, emotional, social, and even financial needs.

“Beloved, I pray that you may prosper in all things and be in health, just as your soul prospers” (3 John 2)”

Our meetings.

We meet on the 3rd Saturday of the Month for prayers: 8 am to 9 am.

We also meet on the last Sunday of the Month. (Immediately after the Worship service).
