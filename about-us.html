FROM OUR PASTOR

Welcome To Our Church

Welcome to the official website of RCCG Lagos Province 85 - Land of Goshen, Overflowing Mega Parish! We are delighted to have you here as a member, visitor, or someone seeking spiritual guidance. Our church, under the esteemed leadership of Pastor Matthew Aiyedun as the Pastor in Charge (PICP) of LP85, is committed to providing a nurturing environment for spiritual growth and a vibrant community where everyone can experience God's love and grace.

RCCG Lagos Province 85 is a dynamic and thriving community of believers located in the heart of Lagos, Nigeria. As part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), one of the largest and fastest-growing Pentecostal churches in the world, we are dedicated to fulfilling the Great Commission by spreading the gospel, making disciples, and impacting lives with the transformative power of Jesus Christ.

Our Children Church is a whole lot of wonderful experience for the various age groups. Our teenagers have their fellowships in an atmosphere that grows them in all spheres of life. In our adult Church, the Word of God works wonders as the whole gospel is preached with God bearing witness to His Word daily. As a community impact church, we carry out regular medical outreach, corporate social responsibility projects, and other support outreaches to reflect the reality of the love of Jesus to all.

We have service platforms for every member to use their gifts and talents to serve God and contribute their quota to the furtherance of the work of God’s Kingdom on earth. We will be glad to have you join us.

“As you go through this site, you will get to know more about this Church and discover your place in it. God has brought you here for a royal purpose and we know that He will see through its fulfillment.”

OUR VISION & MISSION

To spread the Word of God to the ends of the earth.

The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) has the vision to spread the Word of God to the ends of the earth. It is a Bible-believing church, where the teaching and a missionary approach emphasize the proper conduct and moral behaviour of people. The church has an appeal to all types of people worldwide, with a determination to teach people in their various geographical areas, stages of secular life and levels of spiritual development.

Vision

  1. To make heaven.
  2. To take as many people with us.
  3. To be a shining light and a beacon of hope in our community.
  4. Reflecting the love and teachings of Jesus Christ to all.

Mission

  1. Exalt God: We believe in heartfelt worship, praising God for His goodness, mercy, and faithfulness.
  2. Equip Believers: Through biblical teachings, seminars, and discipleship programs, we aim to equip believers with the knowledge and wisdom needed to live victorious lives.
  3. Evangelize the Lost: We are passionate about reaching the lost with the message of salvation and sharing the good news of Jesus Christ with people from all walks of life.
  4. Empower the Community: As a church, we strive to engage in outreach activities, social initiatives, and community development projects that uplift and empower those in need.

F. A. Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is your Sunday worship service?

We hold a single worship service each Sunday in our church auditorium. We start with a Sunday school by 8:00 am followed by a celebration service by 9:00 am. We also hold a monthly thanksgiving service on every first Sunday.

Do I have to belong to your denomination to attend Jesus House?

Not at all! There are many in our congregation that come from a church background other than the RCCG. We are a “patchwork” church – people with different church backgrounds “woven together” by God’s grace.

What kind of things do you do in your worship service?

We strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere at Jesus House. We greet each other so everyone feels welcomed; we share announcements and church news; we sing, dance, and listen to our choir perform beautiful music; we lift the congregation’s joys and concerns to God through thanksgiving and prayer; we read the Bible and talk about it through the sermon; and we collect an offering to further God’s work.

Do you have a parking space in your church?

Yes we do. We have locations around the church premises where your car can be safely parked. We have church volunteers who together with security agencies, will watch over your car while you worship.

Do you hold online services?

Yes, we hold online services. If you are unable to make it to church for any reason, you can join our live services on our livestream page. We make both audio and video broadcasts of our Sunday worship services. At this time, we also hold Bible Studies and Prayer Meetings online via Zoom Meetings. Kindly check our events calendar for the schedules of such meetings.

How do I get more involved in the church??

A perfect place to start is by visiting a small group, which provides an excellent opportunity to get to know more people and grow. Our house fellowships and natural groups are a place to start. You can also take your involvement a step further by joining the church workforce.

What do you offer for children on Sunday morning?

We offer Sunday school and impact classes for children aged 3 years through to 12 years. We also cater to infants, crawlers, walkers, and newborns up to the age of 2 during each of our worship services. Our teachers and minders teach Jesus-centered topics in a fun and engaging way in our secure children’s area which allows you to have peace of mind during services.
