Childrens' Church

Moulding responsible and reliable children
ABOUT US

Who we are.

Jesus House Children’s Church is the Children’s arm of the RCCG Jesus House Parish, catering mainly to children from the ages of (0) to Twelve (12) years. As the Scripture says “Train up a child the way he should go, he will not depart from it when he is old” (Proverbs 22:6); the Children’s church is dedicated to raising a generation of God-fearing, spiritually, morally and socially sound children who would do exploits for God and their nations.

We provide a conducive environment for our children to thrive mentally, physically, and socially taking into cognizance their individual talents gifts and the need to excel in all do.

We are mindful of the fact that helping to mold and shape children into responsible adults is essential at a time like this, hence we provide support and mentoring for children who may be troubled and having difficulties either in their studies or natural environment.

The Church has several activities to keep the children deeply rooted in the word of God, some of these include annual Bible quiz and sporting competitions, annual children’s Easter Retreat, Drama and Choir groups, annual summer classes where we combine academic work with extracurricular activities, various ministrations in singing, drama, poetry and much more.

Our goals

  • To teach Children the undiluted word of God.
  • To help them put their trust in God at all times.
  • To mould them into responsible and reliable Children.
  • To help them discover their calling early in life.
  • o inculcate into them a passion for the Lord.
  • To help them discover and nurture their God-given talent and gifts.
